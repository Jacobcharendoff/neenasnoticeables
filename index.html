hi, I'm Neena, and I make…

gifts you feel before you see

I'm a maker in Toronto. I'm blind, so everything I create — my soaps, my candles, my little keepsakes — is made by feel and by scent. My hands are very picky. If a piece doesn't feel right, it doesn't leave my table.

a little note from me

why my gifts get talked about

Dear friend,

I make things you notice with more than your eyes. I lost most of my sight, and my hands quietly took over the work my eyes used to do. Honestly? They do it better.

It turns out people are a little tired of gifts that come off a shelf, and a little hungry for things made slowly, by an actual person, at an actual kitchen table.

Neena xo
