the idea behind everything

a gift for all five senses

A "noticeable" is the thing most people rush past — and I can't explain noticing to you. I have to hand it to you. Every box is a little tour of the world the way I experience it.

touch

soap cut and judged by hand — made for yours

scent

a candle blended the way some people mix colours

taste

a chocolate I picked myself, for while the candle burns

sound

the strike of the match, the first crackle

sight

the candle's glow. I can't see it — that part of the gift is yours.

why I share these

Dear friend,

When my sight faded, everything else got louder. The weight of a good bar of soap. The way lavender smells different in the morning. The little sound a match makes when it catches. Losing my sight didn't take my senses — it turned the rest of them all the way up.

I put my noticeables in these boxes because you can't explain noticing to someone. You have to hand it to them.

Neena xo
