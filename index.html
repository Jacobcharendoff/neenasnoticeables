hi, I'm Neena, and I make…

gifts you feel before you see

I'm a maker in Toronto. I've been blind since the day I was born, so I've never seen the world — I've always felt it, smelled it, and heard it. Everything I make is judged by my hands, and my hands are very picky. If a piece doesn't feel right, it doesn't leave my table.

the idea behind everything

a gift for all five senses

A "noticeable" is the thing most people rush past — and I can't explain noticing to you. I have to hand it to you. Every box is a little tour of the world the way I experience it.

touch

soap cut and judged by hand — made for yours

scent

beeswax and essential oils — blended for your nose

taste

a chocolate I picked myself, for while the candle burns

sound

the strike of the match, the first crackle

sight

the candle's glow. I've never seen one — that part of the gift is yours.

why I share these

Dear friend,

I was born too early and too small, and the incubator that saved my life took my sight. So I've never seen the world. I've spent my whole life noticing it instead — the weight of a good bar of soap, the way lavender smells different in the morning, the little sound a match makes when it catches.

I put my noticeables in these boxes because I want you to feel what I feel every time someone thinks of me: noticed. You've been thought of — by me — even if nobody else thought of you that day.

Neena xo
