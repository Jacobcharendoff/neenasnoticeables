Three weeks minimum from deposit. For holiday season, book by early November — I make small batches with my own two hands, so I sell out of calendar, not stock.

Yes — ribbon and tags from 25 boxes, full colour treatment from 50.

50% deposit to start (it buys the supplies), balance on delivery. Invoice by email.

GTA delivery is included at 50+ boxes, quoted below that. Shipping across Canada available.
