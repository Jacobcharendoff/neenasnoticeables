good questions

things buyers ask

How far ahead should we order?

Three weeks minimum from deposit. For holiday season, book by early November — I make small batches with my own two hands, so I sell out of calendar, not stock.

Can you put our logo or colours on the gifts?

Yes — ribbon, tags and box colours can match your brand. Custom orders start at 24 pieces.

How does payment work?

50% deposit to start (it buys the supplies), balance on delivery. Invoice by email.

Is there a story we can share internally?

My story card is in every gift, and I'm happy to write a short note for your internal comms — people love knowing whose world their gift came from.

Do you deliver?

Delivery within the GTA and shipping across Canada — quoted with your order.
